Our Services

All IT services for your business

TriPOS is our POS platform, but our team does a lot more than that. We bring hands-on technical expertise to support every part of your business, from building your online presence to setting up hardware, making sense of your data, and tackling whatever tech challenges come up along the way.

01

Web Solutions

We design and build websites that are clean, fast, and built around your brand. Whether you need a simple landing page, a full menu showcase, or an online ordering integration, our team handles the whole thing so you don't have to figure it out yourself.

02

Hardware Integration

Getting the right hardware set up and talking to each other is half the battle. We source, configure, and install printers, payment terminals, tablets, cash drawers, and other devices, and make sure everything works together with TriPOS from day one. If something breaks down the road, we're the ones you call.

03

AI-Driven Solutions

We help you put your data to work. From sales forecasting and customer behavior analysis to inventory optimization and peak-period predictions, our AI tools surface the patterns that are hard to see on your own, so you can act on them before they become problems or missed opportunities.

04

Consultation & Education

Technology is only useful if your team knows how to use it. We run onboarding sessions, staff training, and hands-on walkthroughs to get everyone up to speed quickly. And when questions come up after go-live, we're still here. Not a chatbot, not a ticket queue, just us.

05

Custom Solutions

Sometimes off-the-shelf doesn't cut it. If you have a workflow, integration, or problem that needs something built specifically for your operation, we can do that too. Tell us what you're trying to solve and we'll figure out the best way to build it.
