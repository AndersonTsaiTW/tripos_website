Practical tools that deliver real results for Canadian restaurants, cafés, and retailers.

01

Sales Management

Get a live view of every transaction as it happens, by item, category, staff, or time of day. Daily, weekly, and monthly reports show you exactly where your revenue comes from, which items are your top performers, and when your busiest hours are. All the numbers you need to make faster, smarter calls on the floor.

02

Customer Management

Build real relationships with your regulars. TriPOS stores customer profiles with contact details, visit history, and purchase records so you always know who's walking in the door. Spot your highest-value customers, understand their preferences, and create the kind of experience that brings people back, without any extra effort on your end.

03

Business Analysis

Stop guessing and start knowing. TriPOS turns your sales data into clear, visual insights: trend lines, period comparisons, peak-hour breakdowns, and revenue tracking all in one place. Identify what's working, catch issues early, and walk into every week with a real picture of how your business is performing.

04

Inventory Management

Know exactly what you have and what you're running low on, before service starts. TriPOS tracks stock levels in real time, alerts you when items hit their reorder threshold, and logs usage so you can spot waste and shrinkage fast. Tighter inventory means lower costs, fewer 86'd items, and less stress at the end of the night.
